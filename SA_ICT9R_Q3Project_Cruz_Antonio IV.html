Prevention Tips

Here Are Some Tips To Telp Prevent Fires!

Tips & Tricks How To Do The Tip
Install Smoke Detectors Smoke detectors can alert you to a fire before it's too late. Make sure to install smoke detectors on every floor of your home.
Keep Flammable Materials Away Keep flammable materials such as curtains, paper, and cleaning supplies away from heat sources.
Don't Overload Electrical Outlets Don't overload electrical outlets and make sure to unplug appliances that aren't being used.
